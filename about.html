About vinylworld

‘Welcome all, your satisfaction is our pride.’

We are a subscription-based service that specializes in the sale of vinyl records. We have thousands of vinyl records ready to be sent to you with a button click. We ask about your preference of genre of music, the preference for the period you enjoy your music from and an example of several artists you enjoy. Once the customer gives this information and signs the subscription, they will receive vinyl records from the business every month. They will be selected randomly based on the customers' preferences. The cost of a subscription varies and is very flexible.
